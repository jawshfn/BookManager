Bookstore User Guide

Adding a Book

To add a book to the list, click the 'Add Book' button. Fill in the book's details in the dialog box and click 'Submit'.

Removing a Book

To remove a book from the list, select the book in the list and click the 'Remove Book' button. The book will be removed from the list.

Removing All Books

To remove all books from the list, click the 'Remove All' button. All books will be removed from the list.
