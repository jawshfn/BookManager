Bookstore User Guide

Adding a Book

To add a book to the list, click the 'Add Book' button. Fill in the book's details in the dialog box and click 'Submit'.

Note: the quantity amount must be a positive whole number

Removing a Book

To remove a book from the list, select the book in the list and click the 'Remove Book' button. The book will be removed from the list. If the quantity is > 1, then the quantity will be decreased by one and the book will remain on the list with its new associated quantity.

Removing All

This button is used for when you wish to remove a book from the list no matter the quantity. Ex: If you have a book with a quantity of 100 but want to take it off of the list, you simply click on that book and then click the remove all button, this will remove the book from the list.
